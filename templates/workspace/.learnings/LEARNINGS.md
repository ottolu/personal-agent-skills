# LEARNINGS

> Record only reusable learnings: user corrections, knowledge gaps, better recurring approaches, and workflow improvements.
> Do **not** use this as a work diary.

## Logging Rule
Add an entry only when at least one is true:
- the user clearly corrected you
- you found a better repeatable approach
- a knowledge gap affected output quality
- the same issue is likely to recur

## Template

**Priority**: low | medium | high
**Status**: pending | resolved | promoted
**Area**: behavior | workflow | tool | writing | research | integration
